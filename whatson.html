Each year, the History Council of Western Australia presents a public lecture that shares fresh thinking and new evidence on an historical topic

ANNUAL LECTURE 2024

Opened by Professor Geoffrey Bolton OA in 2004, the Annual Lecture is a public lecture series of original work that constitutes a significant contribution to historical knowledge.

AWARDS

As the peak body representing history in Western Australia, the History Council advances the practice and place of history within all levels of the community. Through two decades of advocacy, the History Council consults and liaises with many groups and organisations on a wide variety of historical issues. Through its events, the History Council brings together those interested in the role of history within Western Australia.

2024 ANNUAL LECTURE

In 2024 the National Indigenous Research Conference and the AIATSIS National Native Title Conference will be held together for the first time. Over five days, the summit will provide a fantastic opportunity for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander policy makers, academics and leaders to come together to discuss emerging challenges. Registrations are now open for this unique event.
